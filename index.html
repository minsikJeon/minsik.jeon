Minsik Jeon

I'm a research scientist at Agency for Defense Development in South Korea.

At Google I've worked on Glass, Lens Blur, HDR+, VR, Portrait Mode, Portrait Light, and Maps. I did my PhD at UC Berkeley, where I was advised by Jitendra Malik. I've received the PAMI Young Researcher Award.

Research Interests

I'm interested in computer vision, Robotics, blahblah. Most of my research is about inferring the physical world (shape, motion, color, light, etc) from images, usually with radiance fields. Representative papers are highlighted.
